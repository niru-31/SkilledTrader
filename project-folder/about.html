Founder Story

The journey behind SkilledTrader

My journey in financial markets started at the age of 19. What began as curiosity slowly became obsession. I spent countless hours studying charts, market structure, derivatives, and trader psychology.

Over time, I built a growing trading community and started sharing educational content online. Within months, hundreds of students joined the community because they wanted clarity in a space filled with confusion.

Then life changed direction. I paused trading and moved toward traditional work opportunities. Slowly, I realised something important — passion disappears when you stop practising it daily.

That realization became the foundation of SkilledTrader. I returned to markets with a bigger mission: helping beginners understand finance properly instead of blindly following social media noise.
