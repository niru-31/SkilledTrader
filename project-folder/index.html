About Me

I didn't just study markets.
I lived them.

At 19, markets were my identity — actively trading FnO & Equity, running a community of 400+ traders, and building a YouTube channel to 1.5K subscribers within months. Then life pulled me toward a job. Trading stopped. The community went silent. And slowly, my edge faded.

That loss taught me something no textbook can: passion fades when you stop practising it. So I resigned, rebuilt, and launched SkilledTrader — a structured education platform for students who deserve clarity, not random chaos.

Most beginners discover trading first and never see the full picture — Investment Advisory, Research Analysis, Portfolio Management. I help them see it all.

Market Insights

Ideas that shape how I teach & trade

📊

Risk-reward matters more than accuracy

3 losses at –1R, 1 win at +5R. 25% accuracy, still profitable. Professionals manage risk-reward ratios — not win rates.

📉

Your 12% return isn't really 12%

After tax (~10%) and inflation (~3–4%), the "good return" becomes 6–7%. Real, after-cost returns build wealth. Most investors never track this.

🤖

AI speeds analysis — it can't replace judgement

Calculators made math faster; they didn't replace mathematicians. AI can summarise reports. Context and financial judgement still come from expertise.

🎓

Teaching is the deepest form of learning

To explain a chart to someone who knows nothing, you must understand it at a level no textbook requires. Every session is training.

🌐

Markets are far bigger than trading

Most beginners see trading and miss the rest — Advisory, Research, Portfolio Management. Understanding the full landscape changes everything.

🧠

Passion fades when you stop practising

Identity built on skill requires daily maintenance. Stepping away — even for a "good job" — quietly erodes the sharpness that took years to build.
